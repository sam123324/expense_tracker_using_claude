Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register for Spendly, we collect your name, email address, and account credentials. As you use the service, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes:

  • Displaying your expense statistics and reports
  • Personalizing your experience within the application
  • Monitoring and maintaining the security of our service

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by our cloud provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may use third-party services to help us operate the application (such as hosting or analytics), but these services are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.com.
